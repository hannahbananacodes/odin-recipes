Morning Oatmeal

Totes mcgoats

This may only be eat in the morning! It is forbidden to eat morning oats at any other time of day. It may be eaten for the following meals: breakfast, second breakfast, elevensies.

Ingredients

  • IngredientAmount
  • Rolled oats1/2 cup
  • Milk1 cup
  • Granola1/4 cup
  • Frozen cherries1/4 cup
  • Blueberries1/4 cup
  • Banana1/4 each

Steps

  1. Add oatmeal and water to bowl, microwave until cooked.
  2. Slice banana.
  3. Top oatmeal with fruit and granola.
